QuipPix transforms your photos into stunning works of art with a single tap. Choose from 15 unique styles — from caricatures to oil paintings, magazine covers to cyberpunk portraits — and customize every detail.

Get 6 creative styles and 5 daily generations completely free. No coins. No forced login. Upgrade to QuipPix Pro for the full experience.

- Comic Book & Pop Art
- Pencil Sketch (Clean & Gritty)
- Watercolor & Oil Painting
- Anime-Inspired
- Cyberpunk Neon
- Magazine Cover (with custom text!)
- Professional Headshot
- Dreamy Portrait & Editorial Fashion

**QUIPPIX PRO**
Unlock all 15 styles, high-res and ultra exports, priority processing, advanced controls (Micro Detail, Studio Relight, Background Pro), and unlimited daily generations.

**FULL CREATIVE CONTROL**
Fine-tune every creation with sliders for intensity, detail, face fidelity, color mood, and more. Toggle identity preservation and skin tone accuracy. Some styles have extra controls — add halftone to comics, custom text to magazine covers, or choose your headshot backdrop.

**ADD YOUR OWN CREATIVE DIRECTION**
Tell the AI exactly what you want: "Make me look like a superhero chef" or "Create a dreamy sunset vibe" — your words shape the result.

**POST DIRECTLY TO SOCIAL**
Share your creations straight to Instagram Stories, Instagram Feed, TikTok, Facebook, X (Twitter), and Snapchat — images auto-sized for each platform.

**SHARE & GO VIRAL**

**PRIVACY FIRST**
No account. No tracking. Photos are processed securely and automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour. All EXIF data is stripped before upload.
