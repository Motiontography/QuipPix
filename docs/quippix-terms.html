02 Description of Service

QuipPix is a photo-to-art transformation application that allows users to upload photographs and transform them into various artistic styles, including but not limited to:

  • Caricatures
  • Oil paintings
  • Watercolors
  • Anime and manga styles
  • And additional artistic styles as made available

The App uses artificial intelligence, specifically OpenAI's image generation API, to process and transform your images. The service is provided on a tiered basis:

  • Free Tier: 5 daily image generations with access to 6 artistic styles.
  • Pro Tier: 30 daily image generations with access to 15 artistic styles, plus larger output sizes and additional features.

Daily generation limits reset every 24 hours. Unused generations do not carry over to the next day.
